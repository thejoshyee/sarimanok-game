# Steam Setup Checklist

> Complete these steps to be ready for Next Fest registration by January 5, 2026.

---

## Phase 1: Account Setup (DO TODAY - Dec 3)

### Step 1: Pay Steam Direct Fee

- [ ] Go to https://partner.steamgames.com
- [ ] Pay $100 Steam Direct fee
- [ ] This creates your Steamworks partner account

### Step 2: Start Identity Verification

- [ ] Submit government-issued ID
- [ ] Wait 2-5 business days for verification
- [ ] Check status daily in Steamworks dashboard

### Step 3: Submit Tax & Bank Info

- [ ] Complete tax interview (W-9 for US, W-8BEN for non-US)
- [ ] Add bank account for payments
- [ ] Wait 1-3 weeks for verification

**Timeline Note:**

- Verification can take 1-4 weeks total
- Starting today (Dec 3) gives buffer for holiday delays (Dec 23 - Jan 1)
- You CANNOT create a store page until verification is complete

## Store Page Requirements Checklist

Before submitting your store page, ensure you have:

### Screenshots (5+ required)

- [ ] Screenshot 1: Sarimanok surrounded by enemies (core gameplay)
- [ ] Screenshot 2: Level-up screen with weapon choices (progression)
- [ ] Screenshot 3: Manananggal boss encounter (excitement/stakes)
- [ ] Screenshot 4: Multiple weapons firing at once (power fantasy)
- [ ] Screenshot 5+: Shop screen, character select, or varied environments
- [ ] All screenshots at 1920Ã—1080 minimum resolution
- [ ] First 4 screenshots show different gameplay aspects (appear in hover preview!)

### Trailer

- [ ] Gameplay visible in first 4-5 seconds (NO logos/title cards at start!)
- [ ] Under 60 seconds total length
- [ ] Shows core mechanics (not tutorials)
- [ ] Only ONE trailer in primary position

### Tags (Set in Steamworks)

Priority order:

1. Roguelite
2. Action Roguelike
3. Bullet Hell
4. Pixel Art
5. Survivors (if available)
6. Single Player
7. Mythology
8. Indie (lower priority - too generic)
