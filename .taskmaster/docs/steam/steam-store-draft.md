- **Manananggal** - The terrifying self-segmenting vampire boss

- **Auto-attacking weapons** - Focus on dodging and positioning
- **Build your power** - Collect XP, level up, choose upgrades
- **Permanent progression** - Spend gold on shop upgrades between runs
- **30-minute runs** - Survive to dawn and achieve victory!

## Early Access Notice

### Why Early Access?

"We want to build this game alongside our community! Early Access lets us get the core experience into your hands while we continue adding more Filipino creatures, weapons, and content based on your feedback."

### How long will Early Access last?

"We plan to be in Early Access for 6-12 months as we add more content and polish based on community feedback."

### What's in the Early Access version?

- Complete 30-minute survival gameplay
- 3 playable characters
- 4 enemy types including a boss
- 6 weapons and 4 passive upgrades
- Story Mode and Endless Mode
- Full save system and progression

### What's planned for the full release?

- More creatures from Filipino mythology (Tikbalang, Kapre, Mambabarang)
- Additional playable characters
- More weapons and weapon evolutions
- Mac and Linux support
- Localization (including Tagalog!)

### Will the price change?

"We may slightly increase the price as we add significant content, but Early Access supporters will always have access to everything."

## Trailer Best Practices

| Rule                          | Why It Matters                                      |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Gameplay in first 4-5 sec     | First trailer is used for hover-over clips          |
| NO logos/title cards at start | Wastes precious seconds - show gameplay immediately |
| Under 60 seconds total        | Attention spans are short - be punchy               |
| ONE trailer in primary slot   | Multiple trailers split attention and add noise     |
| Show core mechanics           | Demonstrate how the game is played, not tutorials   |
