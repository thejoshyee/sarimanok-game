================================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE - CANVAS SIZES
================================================================================

32x32  = Characters, regular enemies, small effects
48x48  = Boss only (Manananggal)
64x64  = Large AOE effects (wing slap, flame wing)
16x16  = Icons, projectiles, pickups


================================================================================
ANIMATION FRAMES
================================================================================

2 frames = Simple bob animation (up/down or slight movement)
3 frames = Effects with start/middle/end
4 frames = Boss only (more complex movement)

All animations are HORIZONTAL strips:
  2 frames at 32x32 = export as 64x32 PNG
  4 frames at 48x48 = export as 192x48 PNG
